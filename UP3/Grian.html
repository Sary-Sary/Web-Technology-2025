Grian

Minecraft YouTuber

Grian is a Hermit and a YouTuber with over 8,730,000 subscribers. He joined Hermitcraft at the start of Season 6. He also is known for his building skills and his reputation as a prankster. His most viewed video is 50 Ways To Mess With Your Friends In Minecraft, amassing 90,165,079 views (90 million).
